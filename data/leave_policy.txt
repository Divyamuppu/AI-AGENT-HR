Employees with less than 2 years of experience are eligible for 12 annual leave days per year.
Employees with 2 or more years of experience are eligible for 18 annual leave days per year.
All employees are eligible for 10 sick leave days per year.
Medical certificate is required if sick leave exceeds 3 consecutive days.
A maximum of 5 unused annual leave days can be carried forward to the next year.
Annual Leave Policy:
• Freshers: 12 days per year
• Employees with 2+ years: 18 days per year
• Carry forward: Max 5 days


Casual Leave Policy

Employees are eligible for 6 casual leave days per year.
Casual leave should be applied at least one day in advance.
Casual leave cannot be taken without prior notice except emergencies.
